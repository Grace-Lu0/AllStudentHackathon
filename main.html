10-hour hackathon for 11th and 12th grade students

Join students to build tools that help others study more effectively — flashcards, planners, AI tutors, or something totally new.

Who should join?

Anyone who wants to build — from beginners to experienced coders. Bring an idea and enough coding knowledge to make something functional.

  • Work solo or with a teammate
  • Prizes for innovation & technical skill
  • Free food, drinks, and a comfortable workspace

Why attend?

Sharpen your coding skills, solve authentic problems, and collaborate with students across grade levels. Great for resumes and college apps.

What to bring:

Laptop & charger

Notepad & Snacks

Project ideas

Flashcard apps, study planners, AI tutors, collaborative study tools, accessibility helpers, or original study aids.
